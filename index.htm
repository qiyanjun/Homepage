Welcome!


I am an associate professor (tenured) at the Department of Computer Science , University of Virginia. Please feel free to call me "Jane", "Yanjun", "Professor Qi" or "Dr. Qi". In 2021, I was part of the National Scholar Program in National Institute of Health, NIA Program.

Projects:

My team focuses on three major research fronts:

  • 1. Our deep learning (DL) tools, focusing on DL generalization ;
  • 2. Our algorithmic tools for making deep learning robust and trustworthy;
  • 3. Our graph learning tools for extracting relational networks from heterogeneous samples.
